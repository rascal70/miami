What to do in Miami

So you're going to Miami for spring break. How amazing! You couldn't have chosen a more beautiful place. The city will take your breath away. Having been born and raised in Miami's Lil' Haiti, I might be somewhat biased, but there are many people who share my sentiments. Get ready for beautiful sky in all its glory. Be prepared for greenery upon greenery, as colors are popping in this town. Ask anyone who has ever visited this place I call home, there is no place like it. Before you embark on the best spring break ever, here are some tips from an insider. You can thank me later. Welcome to Miami.

Before you go, you want to make sure you are properly prepared for what's in store for your trip. Checking the weather for the dates you'll be there will do exactly that. Yes, Miami is hot. Yes, Miami is humid. Yes, Miami will rain down on you with no hesitation. Recently, with the climate shift, it has been "cold for Miami". Therefore, be flexible and check to see what conditions will be in store for you and be ready for whatever.

1. Stroll the beach.

There is always a lot of activity at the beach.

2. Take a bunch of Pictures!

Have the time of your life and make memories. Take photographs and I mean a lot of photographs. No matter what, you'll be having fun. You've planned a trip with some of your favorite people. So get cute and get it poppin'.

3. Get ready to Par-tay!

It's finally happening! You're going on a well-deserved vacation, so have fun.

4. Bon Voyage

Have a safe trip.
